ABOUT US

Branch Studios Hawaii is a fresh, new performing arts school located in the heart of Honolulu.

Our philosophy is to provide engaging lessons in a warm and welcoming environment. We offer courses taught by a young, passionate team of talented teachers.

OUR SCHOOL

We strive to offer the best learning experience in Kaka`ako.

Engaging Lessons

Our highly qualified and caring music teachers utilize innovative teaching methods to ensure that students are learning to their greatest potential.

Collaborative Workshops

We emphasize learning beyond the classroom. Regular workshops feature inspiring artists, performances and additional learning opportunities.

Exceptional Productions

Students are given regular opportunities to develop confidence and share their learning with friends and family.

OUR COURSES

OUR EVENTS

We invite current and prospective studtns to become a part of our thriving musical communtiy.

We believe that there's more to a student's development than a weekly lessons. Our workshops, concerts and gigs are designed to broaden musical knowledge and allow students to meet in a fun yet relaxed setting.
